Research Activity

My research focuses on provably secure E-Democracy (E-Voting, E-Collecting), with a heightened interest in the Swiss setting. My PhD is in formal methods, where I used ProVerif to perform symbolic proofs over cryptographic protocols. I like to work across domains, including cryptography, implementation, and human factors.
